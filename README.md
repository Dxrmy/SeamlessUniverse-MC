**SeamlessUniverse** redefines the spatial architecture of Minecraft. Moving beyond standard load-screen portals, it creates a vertically unified world where dimensions exist as literal layers of a single, continuous universe.

## Features
- **The Nether Below:** The Nether is the literal geological foundation of the world, located deep beneath the Overworld's bedrock layer.
- **The Aether Above:** Seamless sky-access to the Aether (Aether mod compatibility required), placing the heavenly islands directly in the Overworld's skybox.
- **The End / The Abyss:** The End exists as a terrifying vertical abyss, reminiscent of the "Upside Down," waiting beneath the deepest shadows of the world.
- **Zero-Load Transitions:** Travel between dimensions by simply ascending or descending, removing loading screens for a truly immersion-first experience.
- **Inter-dimensional Perspective:** Visual rendering of the layers above and below you from within the Overworld.
